About Us

We are Grade 10 students at APEC Schools. TLE-Life Labs is a subject that aims to provide us with skills and values for developing social innovation through the integration of people, ideas, and resources that promote the practice of universal values. We are studying the negative effects of excessive online gaming on young people.

We do the following to meet the demands of our target audience:

- Survey, in human subjects research, a survey is a set of questions designed to elicit specific information from a specified group of people. Surveys can be performed via the phone, by mail, on the internet, or even on street corners or in shopping malls.

- Research is a systematic inquiry process that includes data gathering, documentation of key information, and analysis and interpretation of that data/information in accordance with appropriate procedures established by certain professional fields and academic disciplines.

- Gather information, from which we determine that all of the results we obtain

When we are finished, we discover that one of the streaming platforms used on the internet is Youtube. YouTube is a video-sharing website that allows users to watch, like, share, comment on, and upload their own videos. The video service is available on PCs, laptops, tablets, and mobile phones. 90% of individuals claim they find new companies or items on YouTube.

And the content that we used is online gaming, because we all know how much attention teenagers and students like us can get from online gaming. One thing we discovered is that online gaming has both positive and negative effects on our physical, mental, and communication skills.

Needs of our Viewers

• According to our study, our users need to address their health during gaming, particularly their weight increase or loss after becoming a gamer.

• One of the concerns that students must address is their behavior toward others, which might influence their socialization and communication skills.

• Time management in relation to gaming and activities is important because we are expected to have time for academic tasks as well as time for rest, which includes playing online games.

• Self-control must be addressed because it can have an impact on our academic performance as well as our attitude toward our teachers and classmates. And we can't manage our time effectively if we don't have self-control.

• Motivation and appreciation, because some teenagers are sensitive or soft-hearted, small things make them cry, but small things make them happy like appreciate them or motivate them.

Students must address their health while gaming, particularly any weight gain or loss that occurs after becoming a video game addict. One of the issues that students must address is their interpersonal behavior.
